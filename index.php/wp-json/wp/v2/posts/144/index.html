Flydubai is making flights during the COVID-19 pandemic a little easier for passengers around the world. Health expenses and quarantine costs are covered if the passenger is diagnosed while traveling between September 1 and December 31, 2020.How the COVID-19 Flight Coverage Works<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The global coverage is a free feature of any ticket booked through the Flydubai website<\/a> or mobile app. It also covers if the ticket is booked through a travel agent, a Flydubai travel shop or the company\u2019s customer center.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

This coverage lasts for 31 days after the first flight included on the itinerary. Even if the passenger continues to travel to another city, coverage remains intact.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Travelers are able to enjoy a comfortable, safe trip without any unforeseen problems. If diagnosed with COVID-19 while traveling, Flydubai covers the first 150,000 euros of medical expenses.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

In addition, some of the quarantine costs are covered as well. If a passenger is placed in quarantine, Flydubai covers up to 100 euros of quarantine expenses per day.Flydubai Makes It Easier to Travel During the COVID-19 Pandemic<\/p>\n\n\n\n
